CORE development process: 

We will be doing the following to build a webapp and mobile app:
1. Research what we'll build on a high level (requirements, tech stack, costs, features, functionality, and more)
2. create a blueprint for the necessary components and funtionality of our project and come up

4. test by going through our work and ensuring we've achieved our goals and removing any unintended outcomes.
